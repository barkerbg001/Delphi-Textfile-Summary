The joys of owning a cat 
What kind of pet are they? 
A cat is loving and attention-seeking. The purring sound they make when they're happy is also very relaxing. It is very comforting to have a pet that becomes glad when you come home after school or work. Their soft fur is pleasing to touch, and it makes them popular with children. 

What can they do for you? 
They can keep you warm with their furry bodies in cold mornings. Some cats have been known to comfort people when they are sick or injured. They also help to get rid of mice and rats 

How are they better than dogs? 
Cats do not create an unpleasant smell
